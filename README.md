<h2 align="center">Cihan Toraman</h2>

<p align="center"><b>Interactive Experience and Systems Designer</b></p>

<p align="center"><i>I design and explore interactive experiences that react and evolve beyond imagination.</i></p>

<br><br>
I design interactive systems and games built on behavior, rules, and interaction rather than scripted outcomes or content-heavy structures. My recent focus is on living ecosystems and non-player-centric design: systems that open up possibilities, evolve on their own, and react in ways no one set up in advance. 

Alongside this, I'm building a framework I call Playgrounds of Reality, which I'll soon share in a form you can read and experience.
